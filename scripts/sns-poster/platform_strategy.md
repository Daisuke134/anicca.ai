## Design System - Build in Public サムネイル

### English Version
```
Viral TikTok / Instagram Reels thumbnail, 9:16 vertical, pure black background, 
huge bold white text perfectly centered: 
"2026/1/XX
Day XX of building Anicca", 
no other text, no color accents, no green, no blue, no gradients, 
only black and white, extremely minimal and high contrast, 
very clean layout that is instantly readable on a phone screen, 
tiny amount of soft white noise texture in the background to avoid looking flat, 
but overall a strict monochrome, calm Buddhist minimalism vibe.
```
